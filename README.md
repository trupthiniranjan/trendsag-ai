# TrendSage AI 🚀

### Transforming Customer Reviews into Real-Time Business Insights

TrendSage AI is an AI-powered customer intelligence platform that analyzes customer reviews and feedback to generate actionable business insights. The platform leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP), Sentiment Analysis, and Machine Learning to help businesses understand customer sentiment, identify emerging trends, and make data-driven decisions.

## ✨ Features

* Sentiment Analysis of customer reviews
* AI-powered Trend Detection
* Multilingual Review Processing
* Spam and Fake Review Detection
* Business Recommendation Engine
* Interactive Analytics Dashboard
* Real-Time Insights and Alerts

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
